The winning strategy for Connect6 involves aligning six pieces of the same color in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Therefore, the basic strategy for playing Connect6 is to maximize the number of your pieces in a row until you reach six in a row and to prevent your opponent from doing the same. In Connect6, the black pieces go first with one move, and then both players alternately make two moves each turn. To win, we need to place our pieces in the best positions for each turn.
